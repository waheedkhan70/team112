Understanding Fraud Types

Phishing Scams

Fraudsters trick users into providing sensitive information through fake emails, texts, or websites that mimic legitimate sources.

Identity Theft

Criminals steal personal information to impersonate victims, often using stolen data to make unauthorized transactions.

Card Skimming

Devices are used to capture card details at ATMs or payment terminals, enabling fraudulent transactions.

UPI Fraud

Fraudsters exploit UPI platforms by sending fake payment requests or hacking accounts to transfer funds.

Account Takeover

Hackers gain access to accounts through stolen credentials, using them to make unauthorized transactions.

Money Laundering

Illegally obtained funds are processed through legitimate accounts to disguise their origin.
